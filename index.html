Why us?

Our school and approach are different from what you may see in other places.
Our main focus is to develop your child physically and mentally, which in turn will help him in achieving more in his future life.

We are not a place where every X months your child will be doing a grading and receiving another colorful belt regardless of his actual level.

No, we don’t sell the belts!

Let’s be honest. If you pay for a grading, your child will pass it and get another belt, but what’s a point in this if is not supported by real skills and knowledge?

We offer classes for kids, where each age group will start with focusing on how to use their body in a safe and more efficient way. Then we move to developing vital motor skills that will flawlessly move into practical applications for later use. After building up these skills, kids will start learning essential self defense techniques.

Besides, there are so many benefits in learning Martial Arts for kids!

For adults we offer to experience traditional martial arts that will keep them fit and active as well as help to achieve different goals, like: self-defense, increased endurance and coordination, stress relief, weight loss and many more!
